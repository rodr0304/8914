Welcome to Empower Ability Labs!

Empower Ability Labs is a hub for learning and empathy-building. We are on a mission to foster understanding and promote inclusive digital experiences for all.

We offer a range of services designed to promote accessibility awareness, drive inclusivity, and enhance the user experience while helping organizations better understand how people with disabilities use technology and navigate the digital world.

Our Approach, Services, and Testimonials

Our Approach

Empower Ability Labs utilizes a hands-on approach to raise awareness and promote empathy. Our immersive workshops and lab days provide participants with a unique opportunity to step into the shoes of individuals with disabilities.

Services

Dedicated spaces and programs designed to cultivate empathy and understanding among individuals towards the challenges faced by people with disabilities in using technology and interacting with various environments.

Empathy Lab Days and Workshops

The lab days and workshops provide hands-on experiences, simulations, and interactions with assistive technologies, tools, and scenarios that replicate the obstacles individuals with disabilities encounter daily.

Inspirational Speakers

Invite a speaker with disabilities to share their unique journey. This addition to your event offers insights that resonate, inspire, educate, and enrich your team’s understanding of inclusion.

Usability Testing

Go beyond WCAG. Involve individuals with disabilities in usability testing to gather valuable insights for refining product strategy and identifying accessibility concerns early, when solutions are more feasible and cost-effective.
